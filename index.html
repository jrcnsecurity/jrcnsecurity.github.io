JRCN Securty Services Inc. Company Profile

Our Vission

JRCN Security Services Inc. is envisioned as a highly professional, responsive and client-oriented security agency dedicated to protecting the lives and properties of its clients to the best of its capability in accordance with the laws of the republic of the Philippines.

Our Mission

Its primary mission is to provide highly trained professional security personnel committed to the protection of the lives and properties of valued clients.

Business Information

JRCN Security Services Inc. was established as a Corporation by a group of professionals that are experts in the field of security, and with License to Operate No. PSA-WGS-M00045-2024 issued and having registered with the Philippine National Police Civil Security Group, in accordance with the provisions of the Republic Act 11917 entitled “The Private Security Services Industry Act”, as amended by Presidential Decree Number 11,100 and 1919. JRCN Security Services Inc., Head Office is located at #5 12th St. cor. Gallagher St., East Tapinac, Olongapo City with branch office located at #70 Diego Silang St., Baguio City.

Our goal is to set the phase in the professionalization of the Security Industry in accordance with the RA 11917 otherwise known as "The Private Security Services Industry Act", and with strict adherence with the Labor Laws and other Government mandatory requirements such as the Social Security Service (SSS), the Pag-Ibig Fund, Philhealth, and also Life/Accident Insurance to ensure fundamental benefits of the security guards and their beneficiaries.

Through with humble beginnings, the company boasts with capabilities that are at par with the most recognized Security Agencies today, and aims to be the best provider of customized security services nationwide in the years to come.

We guarantee excellent services accustomed to every client need, given our wide exposure to all facets of industrial and commercial security, and we aim to surpass the level of your expectation.

It is our conviction that client satisfaction should be given paramount importance. Mainly the reason why human resource is considered as our best asset and places greatest emphasis on staff development and continuous training of security personnel under competent trainers from the PNP, other Law Enforcement Agencies and from the private sector who are specialists in various aspects of security operations.

Deeply relying on its consummate skills, network and experience gained by its pillars through prolific years of service in the security industry in our country, JRCN Security Services Inc. is heading to the future with full confidence that it can face and surpass any challenge that may come along the way.
